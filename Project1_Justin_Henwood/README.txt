@author henw0011@algonquinlive.com


Status
This project uses SFML to display 3 rotating windmills with an animated background and accompanying audio track.  Pressing the 1, 2, or 3 keys 
makes the corrisponding windmill around its axis.  Pressing 4 causes all 4 windmills to orbit around the center plane.  Finally, pressing t causes
the windmills to translate to random positions.
